What is Astronomy?

Astronomy is the study of the heavens. Whether that be simply looking up and observing, or making and using specialized equipment, anyone can be an astronomer. Amateur astronomers are the average backyard hobbyists; people who observe without equipment, with binoculors, affordable or invested in a telescope.

Paper Publication

There are many books and magazines that offer information on astronomy. A good basic reference book is National Geographic's Pocket Guide to the Night Sky of North America. Amore detailed reference book is National Audubon Society's Field Guide to the Night Sky. For quick reference to current events, such as meteor showers and other astrological events, Astronomy Magazine is a good magazine and website.


Software

There are many different kinds of software that astronomers can use. There are many smartphone applications that can be used, as well as ranges of computer software. A well known computer software is called StarryNight. They offer different levels of software depending on what the user needs; from basic to professional, you can find one that suits your needs.
