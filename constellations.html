What are constellations?

Constellations are identifiable star patterns that can be seen at night. Well known ones include The Big Dipper and Orion. Constellations are mainly drawn using the brightest stars, with one being the Alpha Star.

The Zodiac

The Zodiac are the twelve constellations that the sun passes through the equatorial line. Depending on what time of year it it, the sun will be in one of the twelve constellations. This is also the basis for Astrology and Sun Signs.
